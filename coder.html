I took to it quickly, spending hours out of class playing with the concepts I was learning during lessons. I found I had a real passion for software development, particularly in the front end.

For group projects I worked together with my team to develop our complicated ideas into workable Javascript. I stepped in to help develop the HTML and CSS for the project when I could to make it more presentable. I designed and coded this portfolio through a number of iterations until I got it looking how I wanted it to. Most importantly, I enjoy it!

I've a lot more to learn and I'm eager to do so.
