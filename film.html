I've had an interest in music since I was a child, trying lessons on violin, trombone, keyboard and saxophone, before settling on guitar, which I have been playing for more than half my life at this point. I properly started writing and recording music when I was sixteen and enrolled in a Music Technology course at St. Helens College. There my teachers gave me some software and I quickly started making low quality recording of the many instrumentals I came up with on guitar.

I had a few attempts and completing proper releases under the pseudonym, Maniacal Laughter, but it would be a few years until, while at the University of Salford I would release my first proper single as Dr. Nowt. In 2012 "Hello" was produced for my dissertation, alongside a music video, promotional materials and a website (not nearly as nice a website as this one. See the 'Coder' section). Shortly after graduating I wrote and produced my first EP, "Population Control" essentially as an exercise in testing my vocal ability.

It took me a long time to get there, but now that I'd released my first EP and had some confidence in my vocals, I started releasing music much more regularly. Between 2012 and now, I have released an EP or an album every year but two, those two years being when I was traveling.

In 2022 I started performing live, my second gig being at Manchester Punk Festival, and in the summer I realeased a video EP of a live recording from my practice space. In February of 2023 I released a new single, "Top Hat", from an upcoming EP. The rest of the year holds more gigging and recording for Dr. Nowt, and I hope to begin recording a new album later in the year.

Outside of this project, I am beginning to persue other areas of music, such as scoring and creating stock music under the name Nash Matthews. I have always enjoyed adding orchestral instrumentation into my music and have become quite adept at composing for these instruments. I hope to build a collection of instrumental tracks for use in short films, Youtube videos, etc. and eventually write scores for film.

To the right you can find examples of my work, including the Dr. Nowt Spotify page, a Nash Matthews instrumental track, and my 2022 video EP, "Live from the Third Room".
