I have long been interested in film and had a desire to make my own films and videos. During my time at university, I developed a particular interest in video editing. In my second year, some friends and I entered a recording of a local band into a contest run by BBC Introducing and won. I have since made several music videos and shorts, including travel videos, sketches, and a very well received short film, Borrigan (basically a 12-minute Peep Show reference).
I have experience working as an editor in a freelance capacity, creating videos as accompaniments for theatrical performances, as well as music videos.

To the right, you can see a selection of videos I have worked on, largely filming, directing, acting and editing them, including some music videos I have worked on recently, in early 2025.
